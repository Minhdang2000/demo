Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one off my favorite foods. Here is a receipt for deliciously rich scrambled eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter is a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a howl
  3. Once butter has melted add scream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds
